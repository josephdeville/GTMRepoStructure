# Situational Changes

Catalog of business changes that signal buying intent. These are the "what has changed in the business that means they need us now" triggers.

---

## High-Signal Changes

Changes strongly correlated with purchase intent based on historical data.

### Funding Events

| Change | Signal Strength | Best Timing | How to Detect | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|---------------|-------|
| Series A raised | High | 60-90 days post | Crunchbase, news | Building processes |
| Series B raised | High | 30-60 days post | Crunchbase, news | Scaling team |
| Series C+ raised | Medium | 30-60 days post | Crunchbase, news | Enterprise features |

### Leadership Changes

| Change | Signal Strength | Best Timing | How to Detect | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|---------------|-------|
| New VP of Sales | High | 30-90 days post | LinkedIn, news | Building their stack |
| New CRO | High | 30-90 days post | LinkedIn, news | Evaluating everything |
| New CEO | Medium | 60-120 days post | LinkedIn, news | Strategic initiatives |

### Growth Signals

| Change | Signal Strength | Best Timing | How to Detect | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|---------------|-------|
| Rapid hiring (50%+ YoY) | High | During growth | LinkedIn data | Scaling pains |
| New market expansion | High | Announcement | Press releases | New requirements |
| Product launch | Medium | Pre-launch | News, social | Supporting initiatives |

### Technology Changes

| Change | Signal Strength | Best Timing | How to Detect | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|---------------|-------|
| New CRM implementation | High | During/after | Job postings, news | Stack building |
| Legacy system sunset | High | 6-12 months prior | News, job postings | Replacement needed |
| Competitor switch | High | Any time | Direct outreach | Dissatisfaction |

## Medium-Signal Changes

Changes that indicate potential interest but require validation.

| Change | Signal Strength | How to Detect | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|-------|
| Job postings for relevant roles | Medium | Job boards | Indicates team building |
| Conference attendance | Medium | Event lists | Shows topical interest |
| Content engagement | Medium | Marketing data | Early interest signal |
| Website visits | Medium | Intent data | Researching solutions |

---

## Low-Signal Changes

Changes to monitor but not prioritize for outreach.

| Change | Signal Strength | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|-------|
| Office relocation | Low | Operational change |
| Minor leadership shuffles | Low | Internal reorganization |
| Industry event participation | Low | General awareness |

## Detection Methods

### Automated Monitoring
- **Crunchbase** - Funding events
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** - Job changes, hiring
- **Google Alerts** - News mentions
- **Intent data providers** - Website visits, content consumption

### Search-Based Research
Use serp.dev, OpenWebNinja, or similar for:
- "[Company name] raises funding"
- "[Company name] hires VP"
- "[Company name] expansion announcement"
- "[Company name] [competitor] switch"

### Prompts for Research

**Finding triggers for target accounts:**
> "For each account in the target list, search for recent news about:
> - Funding announcements
> - Leadership changes
> - Expansion news
> - Product launches
> Return any situational changes detected with dates and sources."

**Validating trigger signals:**
> "Review our closed-won deals and identify what publicly identifiable changes were happening at those businesses when they were closing. Categorize by trigger type and calculate correlation with deal success."
